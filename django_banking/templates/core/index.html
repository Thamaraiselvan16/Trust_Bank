TrustBank

For my project, I developed an application called TrustBank. This application includes features such as depositing and withdrawing amounts, viewing balance history, and user registration and login pages. The deposit function allows users to securely add funds to their account, while the withdraw function enables them to take out funds as needed. The balance history feature provides a detailed record of all transactions, ensuring users can easily track their financial activity. Additionally, the registration and login pages ensure that only authorized users can access their accounts, maintaining the security and integrity of their personal information. Overall, TrustBank offers a comprehensive and secure solution for managing personal finances.
